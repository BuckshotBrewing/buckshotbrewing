Privacy Policy

Effective date:

What we collect

  • When you join the list: your email address, and optionally your first name.
  • When you contact us: your name, email, company, and message content.

Where your information goes today

  • Join the list: if we have not connected an email provider yet, your entry may be stored only in your browser on this device (local storage) and not sent to Buckshot Brewing.
  • Contact: the contact form may open your email app and send to sales@buckshotbrewing.com. The site does not store that message.

How we use information

  • To share launch updates and announcements once an email provider is connected.
  • To respond to retail, distributor, and media inquiries.
  • To improve the site experience.

Sharing

We do not sell personal information. If we connect an email platform later (for example Mailchimp or ConvertKit), your email will be stored with that provider so we can send updates.

Unsubscribe

Once an email provider is connected, each email will include an unsubscribe link.
